FAQ

Common Questions

Do you provide drone services across the full DFW area?

Yes. APS Drone serves Dallas, Fort Worth, Arlington, Plano, Frisco, Irving, and nearby DFW cities.

Are you FAA certified for commercial drone work?

Yes. APS Drone operates under FAA Part 107 commercial drone rules.

How do I request a quote for drone photography or video?

Use the quote form below or email setenaytaftaf@gmail.com with location, timeline, and project goals.

What are your starting prices?

Typical starting rates are real estate photos from $249, aerial video from $499, FPV tours from $1,199, and thermal inspections from $1,999. Final pricing depends on scope and location.

How fast is delivery after the shoot?

Most projects are delivered in 24 to 48 hours, depending on the number of files and edit requirements.

Do you serve all of DFW?

Yes. We work across the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex including Dallas, Fort Worth, Arlington, Plano, Frisco, and nearby cities.

What happens if weather is not fly-safe?

We reschedule quickly when needed for high wind, rain, or poor visibility to maintain safe FAA-compliant operations and media quality.

Can you fly near controlled airspace?

Yes, when authorized. We handle required airspace authorization steps for eligible projects.

Do you offer recurring construction progress flights?

Yes. Weekly, biweekly, and monthly progress capture plans are available for builders and project managers.

What deliverables do I receive?

Deliverables can include edited still photos, short-form and long-form video, social-ready clips, thermal captures, and project-specific exports.

Do you provide FPV drone tours?

Yes. We use DJI Avata for immersive indoor and action-style fly-through videos for commercial and event spaces.

Can you work with agencies and marketplaces?

Yes. We regularly complete assignments for marketplace and agency workflows, including standard upload and delivery requirements.
