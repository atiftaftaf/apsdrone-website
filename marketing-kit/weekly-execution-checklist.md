# Weekly Execution Checklist

## Monday
- Add 50 DFW prospects to `target-list-template.csv`
- Prioritize by segment: Realtors, builders, roofers, property managers
- Send first-touch emails to top 25

## Tuesday
- Send first-touch emails to next 25
- Send 10 Instagram DMs to warm local prospects

## Wednesday
- Send Follow-up #1 to Monday prospects
- Publish one Google Business Profile post (new photo/video + service area text)

## Thursday
- Send Follow-up #1 to Tuesday prospects
- Make 10 short call attempts to warm prospects

## Friday
- Send Follow-up #2 to previous week leads
- Request 5 new Google reviews from recent clients
- Update pipeline statuses and next follow-up dates

## KPI Targets
- 50 new prospects/week
- 50 first-touch emails/week
- 30+ follow-ups/week
- 5 new reviews/month minimum
- 3-8 qualified conversations/week
